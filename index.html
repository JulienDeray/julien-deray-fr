Resources

Leader's Tools

Books, methods, and frameworks that shaped how I lead. A living collection — I add to it as I discover things that work.

📚 Books

📖

The Manager's Path

Camille Fournier

The canonical guide to engineering leadership — from tech lead to CTO. I re-read a chapter whenever I face a new challenge.

leadership
💬

Radical Candor

Kim Scott

Care personally, challenge directly. The framework that taught me to give feedback without being a jerk or avoiding hard conversations.

feedback
🚀

Accelerate

Nicole Forsgren et al.

The data behind high-performing teams. DORA metrics, deployment frequency, and what actually moves the needle.

metrics
🏗️

Team Topologies

Skelton & Pais

How to structure teams and interactions for fast flow. Changed how I think about Conway's Law and organizational design.

org design
🧩

An Elegant Puzzle

Will Larson

Systems thinking applied to engineering management. Org sizing, hiring, and the art of saying no.

systems
🧠

Thinking, Fast and Slow

Daniel Kahneman

Not a management book, but the best book on decision-making I've read. Biases affect hiring, planning, and every 1:1.

decisions

🛠️ Methods & Frameworks

🔄

Scrum & Kanban

Certified in both. Scrum for structured sprints. Kanban for continuous flow. Most teams need a hybrid.

agile
🎯

OKRs

Objectives and Key Results done right. The art is picking 2-3 objectives that force tradeoffs, not 15 that say yes to everything.

goals
🤝

1:1 Templates

A good 1:1 has a rhythm: personal check-in, career growth, blockers, feedback. I keep a shared doc per direct report — never take notes in secret.

people
📋

RFC Process

Write before you build. A lightweight RFC culture catches bad designs in hours, not sprints. Template: Context, Proposal, Alternatives, Decision.

tech
🔄

Retrospectives

The most underrated ritual. The 4Ls framework (Liked, Learned, Lacked, Longed for) surfaces emotions alongside actions.

teams
📊

DORA Metrics

Four numbers that tell you if your team is healthy: deployment frequency, lead time, change failure rate, time to restore.

metrics
